Mei Ling Lee Dance Group

About us

In the August of 1992, the Mei-Ling Lee Chinese Dance Group came together, under supervision of Mei-Ling Lee, to perfect Chinese folk dancing for enlightenment of the western viewer. To date, they have performed at:

  • Southern California at International Folk Dance Festivals
  • Huntington Beach Cultural Kaleidoscopes
  • Irvine Harvest Festivals
  • Orange County Performing Arts Center
  • Beverly Hills Feather Award presentations
  • Knotts Berry Farm
  • Disneyland
  • Orphanages
  • Internal Revenue Services
  • University of California Irvine
  • Postal Association of Southern California
  • Soka University International Festival
  • Irvine Global Village Festival
  • World Dance performance at Orange Coast College
  • Segerstrom Center for the Arts
  • Festival of Children at South Coast Plaza
  • and many more...

As a choreographer, Mei-Ling has incorporates the ceremonial customs of her homeland with the movements of dance in a delightful and entertaining way.
